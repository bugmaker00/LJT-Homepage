**On the Perception Bottleneck of VLMs for Chart Understanding**  
Junteng Liu, Weihao Zeng, Xiwen Zhang, Yijun Wang, Zifei Shan, Junxian He  
*arXiv preprint arXiv:2503.02425 (2025)*  

[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.02425) | [Code](https://github.com/Vicent0205/Vision4Chart)  

We systematically investigate the **perception bottleneck** of Vision-Language Models (VLMs) in chart understanding. Our analysis reveals that current VLMs exhibit significant deficiencies in fine-grained visual perception of chart elements, which fundamentally limits their chart comprehension ability. We provide insights and directions for building more robust chart-understanding VLMs.
