**SynLogic: Synthesizing Verifiable Reasoning Data at Scale for Learning Logical Reasoning and Beyond**  
Yuanxiang Fan, Zhuo Jiang, Han Ding, Yongyi Hu, Chi Zhang, Yiqi Shi, Shitong Weng, Aili Chen, Shiqi Chen, Yunan Huang, Mozhi Zhang, Pengyu Zhao, Junjie Yan, **Junteng Liu**, Junxian He  
*arXiv preprint arXiv:2502.14723 (2025)*  

[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.14723) | [Code](https://github.com/Vicent0205/SynLogic)  

**SynLogic** is a framework for synthesizing **large-scale verifiable logical reasoning data** at scale. By leveraging symbolic solvers to automatically generate and verify reasoning problems, SynLogic enables LLMs to learn complex logical reasoning through self-improvement on a diverse set of automatically verifiable tasks.
