We present SynLogic, a novel method for synthesizing verifiable reasoning data at scale. The approach enables learning logical reasoning and beyond by generating high-quality synthetic training data for large language models. SynLogic improves the logical reasoning capabilities of LLMs while maintaining scalability and verifiability.
