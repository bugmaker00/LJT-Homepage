**Composing Parameter-Efficient Modules with Arithmetic Operations**  
Jinghan Zhang, Shiqi Chen, Junteng Liu, Junxian He  
*Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS 2023)*  

[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.07377)  

We propose a simple yet effective approach to **compose parameter-efficient modules (PEMs)** using arithmetic operations. This method allows flexible combination of multiple specialized adaptations without modifying the base model parameters, achieving strong performance on downstream tasks with minimal additional cost.
