**C-Eval: A Multi-Level Multi-Discipline Chinese Evaluation Suite for Foundation Models**  
Yuzhen Huang, Yuzhuo Bai, Zhihao Zhu, Junlei Zhang, Jinghan Zhang, Tangjun Su, Junteng Liu, Chuancheng Lv, Yikai Zhang, Jiayi Lei, Yao Fu, Maosong Sun, Junxian He  
*Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS 2023)*  

[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.10229) | [Code](https://github.com/SJTU-IPADS/C-Eval)  

**C-Eval** is the first multi-level, multi-discipline Chinese evaluation suite for foundation models. It covers 52 diverse disciplines and four difficulty levels, providing a comprehensive benchmark to evaluate the Chinese knowledge and reasoning capabilities of LLMs.
