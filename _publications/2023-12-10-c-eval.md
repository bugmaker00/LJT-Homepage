C-Eval is the first comprehensive Chinese evaluation suite for foundation models, covering 52 diverse disciplines across multiple difficulty levels. The benchmark reveals significant gaps between current LLMs and human performance on Chinese knowledge and reasoning tasks, providing a valuable tool for evaluating and improving foundation models.
