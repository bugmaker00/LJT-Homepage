Overview
-
I am Junteng Liu, a first-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the HKUST NLP Group, supervised by Professor Junxian He. My research lies in natural language processing and machine learning, with interests in LLM reasoning and reinforcement learning, hallucination in vision-language models (VLMs), and LLM truthfulness and interpretability.
